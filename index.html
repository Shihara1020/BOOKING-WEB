Nearby Attractions

Explore the best Gampola has to offer

2 hour drive

Nuwaraeliya

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as “Little England,” is a charming town in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. Known for its cool climate, colonial-era architecture, and stunning tea plantations, it's a perfect getaway for nature lovers. Explore scenic waterfalls, lush gardens, and serene lakes that capture the heart of Sri Lankan hill country.

8AM-10PM Free Entry
40 min drive

Kandy Town

Kandy Town, nestled in Sri Lanka’s lush hill country, offers a blend of cultural charm and natural beauty. Tourists can explore the iconic Temple of the Tooth, enjoy a peaceful stroll around Kandy Lake, witness traditional dance performances, or visit the Royal Botanical Gardens in nearby Peradeniya. With its vibrant markets, colonial architecture, and scenic train rides, Kandy is a must-visit destination for culture and nature lovers alike.

9AM-5PM $5 Entry
20 min drive

Ambuluwawa

Ambuluwawa is a unique multi-religious hilltop complex located near Gampola, Sri Lanka. Known for its striking white spiral tower, it offers panoramic 360-degree views of mountains, forests, and rivers. Visitors can climb the tower for a thrilling experience and enjoy the peaceful environment surrounded by nature and biodiversity.

Open 24/7 Free Entry
30 min drive

Botanical Park

Beautiful green space in the city perfect for picnics, jogging or simply relaxing.

6AM-10PM Free Entry
1 hour drive

Temple of Tooth

The Temple of the Tooth in Kandy is one of Sri Lanka’s most sacred Buddhist sites. It houses the revered relic of Lord Buddha’s tooth and is a place of deep spiritual significance. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and daily rituals, the temple draws pilgrims and visitors from around the world.

Open 24/7 Free Entry
